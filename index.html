Penguin Habitats

Penguins are primarily found in the Southern Hemisphere, with the highest concentration in Antarctica. However, some species inhabit temperate regions, and the Galapagos Penguin lives near the equator.

  • Antarctica and Subantarctic Islands
  • South America (Chile, Argentina, Peru)
  • Africa (South Africa, Namibia)
  • Australia and New Zealand
  • Galapagos Islands
